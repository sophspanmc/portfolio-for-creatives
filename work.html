  • About Me

    I'm a Vancouver-based illustrator and animation nerd. I have a knack for visualizing concepts, idea generation and shape language. If there’s anything I love more than art, it’s stories. I strive to create narratives wherever possible, crafting a funny recounting of an event for her friends, writing story concepts in coffee shops, shooting the occasional short film and doodling little comics.
