Regulus Rex and the Coral Crown

Character and weapon design of an underwater medieval turtle kingdom

The kingdom of Rex is a quiet one. Ruled over by a fair and just ruler, Regulus, the land has only known peace. Then, one day, a foreign empress arrives at the castle gates, demanding the return of a sacred object - claiming it as her ancestor's and accusing the Rex family of having stolen it. All eyes turn to Regulus. Will he make the right choice? Or will his insistence on honouring tradition, abiding by history and living in entitlement risk the very turtles he had vowed to protect?
